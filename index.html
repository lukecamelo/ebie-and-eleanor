Who we are

Hi, I’m Ebie, and that’s me and Eleanor in the picture there; she’s on the right. We’ve been best friends since we were roommates in college at Agnes Scott University in 198X. We’ve been doing crafts together for almost 30 years, and our latest endeavor, bracelets made from fully recycled materials and repurposed leather, is our most expansive yet. We work very hard on making them, and we think that they all have come out excellent! Take a look, and feel free to shoot us an email using the form at the bottom! Thanks.
